FOG OF WAR
- LoS upgradeable

ARMIES
- Max 3 (upgradeable) units per army, have to be of same type
- When attacked, damage divided evenly
- When attacking, damage = sum of individual units

GATHERING
- Max 3 villagers per resource, upgradeable
- Build mines first on stone, gold, iron

ECONOMY STRUCTURE
- Units cost gold once
- Food cost per turn for each unit
- Need buildings for each unit type to support them (a TC per 5 vills, rax per 5 infantry etc)
